The Equality Act 2010

Equal rights to accessing online content for disabled users is covered by the Equality Act 2010.


Under the Act duty holders must make “reasonable adjustments” to prevent “substantial disadvantage” by ensuring “information is provided in an accessible format”. Organisations providing a service to the public “must not discriminate against a person requiring the service”


The Act also places requirements on public authorities to:

  • eliminate discrimination
  • advance equality of opportunity; and
  • foster good relations between those who are protected, such as disabled
  • or older users and those who are not.
